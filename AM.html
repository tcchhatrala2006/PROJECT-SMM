What is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate Marketing is a type of online marketing where a person or partner (called an affiliate) promotes a product, service, or event and earns a commission for each sale, registration, or action they generate.

In event management, affiliate marketing helps expand reach, attract the right audience, and increase revenue without spending heavily on advertising.

Benefits of Affiliate Marketing

Cost-Effective

Pay only when you get results like ticket sales or registrations.

Wider Reach

Reach new audiences through affiliate partners and influencers.

Higher Conversions

Affiliates promote to targeted audiences, increasing conversions.

Brand Awareness

Improve visibility and credibility of your events.
