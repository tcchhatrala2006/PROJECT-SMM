Reputation Management Plan

Online reputation is critical for Event Management companies because clients rely on reviews and testimonials. Our reputation management strategy includes:

✔ Collecting Google reviews after every event
✔ Showcasing testimonials and success stories on the website
✔ Responding professionally to negative feedback
✔ Monitoring brand mentions on social media
✔ Publishing case studies of successful events

App Store Optimization (ASO) – If Event App is Available

If EventBoost launches a mobile app for event booking and invitations, ASO will help increase downloads. The strategy includes optimizing app title, description, keywords, screenshots, and user reviews in Google Play Store and Apple App Store.
